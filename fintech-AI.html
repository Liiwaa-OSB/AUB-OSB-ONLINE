The Certificate

The Online Certificate in FinTech and AI at the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business, American University of Beirut, is designed as a focused professional credential to meet the growing demand for expertise in financial technologies and artificial intelligence.

The certificate targets a broad ecosystem of professionals and stakeholders interested in understanding the impact of FinTech and AI on financial services, business operations, regulation, and digital transformation. It is designed for individuals across finance, technology, consulting, legal, compliance, entrepreneurship, operations, and related industries seeking practical knowledge of the evolving digital financial landscape.

The certificate emphasizes the role of digital transformation in finance, covering areas such as blockchain applications, cryptocurrencies, digital payments, mobile banking, and AI-driven financial services. Through interactive online lectures and case studies, participants will gain the ability to critically analyze innovations in FinTech, assess their risks and opportunities, and explore entrepreneurial applications.

This certificate is a pivotal stepping stone for professionals aiming to advance their careers, adapt to the fast-changing financial landscape, or explore entrepreneurial opportunities within the FinTech sector.

Why This Certificate

INTERACTIVE, PRACTICAL & INDUSTRY-RELEVANT
Real-life case studies and top-tier faculty and experts.

Explore practical applications of FinTech in the Middle East and engage with hands-on industry-relevant case studies reflecting current trends in FinTech, including digital transformation and national strategies. Led by top-tier faculty and leaders in the industry.

GLOBALLY CONNECTED
Networking opportunities through AUB's global connections.

Access to AUB alumni network: Connect with a vast network of professionals, alumni, and faculty to enhance career prospects.

Industry events and seminars: Participate in events that provide exposure to leading FinTech experts and potential employers.

SHORT, EFFECTIVE & FLEXIBLE
Intensive and asynchronous 2–3 month format.

Accelerated learning experience tailored for different profiles, delivering comprehensive FinTech and AI knowledge through focused, practical, and flexible learning.

Certificate Instructor

Assem Safieddine

Finance, Accounting & Managerial Economics

Dr. Safieddine holds a Ph.D. in Finance from Boston College. His research has been published in leading international finance journals, widely cited by renowned scholars, and featured in prominent academic and business publications. His expertise includes risk management, capital acquisition, mergers and acquisitions, cost of capital, and corporate governance.
Dr. Safieddine has held academic appointments at Michigan State University, Harvard University Summer School, and the American University of Beirut (AUB), where he has served since 2003. At AUB, he held leadership roles including Associate Dean of Academic Programs and Faculty Affairs and Chair of the Finance, Accounting, and Managerial Economics Track. He is a sought-after speaker on finance topics and has advised leading international organizations, government entities, and multinational corporations on corporate governance and financial restructuring.

WHO SHOULD ENROLL?

Open to learners from all backgrounds interested in how FinTech and AI are transforming the financial ecosystem.

You are:
  1. Finance and banking professionals seeking to understand emerging financial technologies and AI applications.
  2. Business leaders and managers driving digital transformation within their organizations.
  3. Technology and IT professionals interested in FinTech innovation and AI-powered solutions.
  4. Consultants, legal advisors, and compliance professionals navigating evolving financial regulations and digital ecosystems.
  5. Entrepreneurs and start-up founders exploring opportunities in FinTech and digital financial services.
  6. Operations and strategy professionals aiming to leverage technology for efficiency and innovation.
  7. Recent graduates and career switchers looking to build future-ready skills in a rapidly growing field.
  8. Professionals across industries who want to stay competitive in the evolving digital economy.
PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS
  • Participants come from different sectors and industries
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English is necessary
  • Valid academic or professional qualifications
