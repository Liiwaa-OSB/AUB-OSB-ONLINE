About the Program

The Aviation Management certificate, developed through the collaboration between the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business (OSB) at the American University of Beirut (AUB) and International Air Transport Association (IATA), combines academic excellence with industry-relevant training to prepare professionals for the evolving demands of the global aviation sector. Designed to address growing workforce needs in Lebanon and the wider MENA region, the certificate equips participants with the practical knowledge, strategic insights, and internationally aligned competencies required to excel in aviation management, safety, security, and revenue optimization.

Delivered through an in-person format, the program blends interactive instruction, real-world case studies, practical exercises, and exposure to global aviation best practices. Participants gain a comprehensive understanding of airline and airport operations, aviation safety and security regulations, and financial and operational decision-making within the aviation industry. Upon successful completion, participants receive an AUB certificate in addition to IATA certificates for the two IATA-certified courses, providing a strong competitive advantage in the aviation job market.
