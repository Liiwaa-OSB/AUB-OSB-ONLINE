About the Program

The future of aviation belongs to leaders who can think globally, lead strategically, and adapt rapidly. Take off toward a future in aviation with a program designed to help you lead, innovate, and succeed.

The Aviation Management certificate, developed through the collaboration between the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business (OSB) at the American University of Beirut (AUB) and International Air Transport Association (IATA), combines academic excellence with industry-relevant training to prepare professionals for the evolving demands of the global aviation sector. Designed to address growing workforce needs in Lebanon and the wider MENA region, the certificate equips participants with the practical knowledge, strategic insights, and internationally aligned competencies required to excel in aviation management, safety, security, and revenue optimization.

Delivered through an in-person format, the program blends interactive instruction, real-world case studies, practical exercises, and exposure to global aviation best practices. Participants gain a comprehensive understanding of airline and airport operations, aviation safety and security regulations, and financial and operational decision-making within the aviation industry. Upon successful completion, participants receive an AUB certificate in addition to IATA certificates for the two IATA-certified courses, providing a strong competitive advantage in the aviation job market.

Why This Program?

Unique & Regionally Competitive
The Aviation Management certificate is one of a kind in Lebanon and among the few specialized aviation management programs offered in the MENA region at a competitive cost, providing participants with accessible, high-quality aviation education aligned with international standards.
Industry-Relevant & Future-Oriented
The certificate addresses the latest trends and challenges shaping the aviation industry, including safety and security compliance, operational efficiency, and airline revenue optimization.
Practical & Career-Focused
Participants gain hands-on exposure through case studies, interactive discussions, and practical applications designed to strengthen real-world decision-making and problem-solving skills.
Globally Recognized
The program combines AUB's academic excellence with IATA's internationally recognized certifications, offering participants both academic credibility and industry-recognized credentials.
Comprehensive Yet Flexible
Designed for working professionals and aspiring aviation leaders, the certificate can be completed within 8 months.
Highly Engaging Learning Experience
The in-person format promotes direct interaction with instructors, peer collaboration, networking opportunities, and engagement with aviation professionals and experts.

Who Should Enroll?

Aviation Professionals

Seeking to strengthen their expertise and advance their careers.

Airline & Airport Managers

Responsible for operations, safety, and strategic decision-making.

Safety & Security Officers

Aiming to stay aligned with international standards and best practices.

Regulatory Professionals

Working in aviation authorities and civil aviation organizations.

Aspiring Aviation Leaders

Preparing for managerial and supervisory roles within the industry.

Recent Graduates

In aviation, transportation, engineering, business, or related fields seeking specialized training.

Career Changers

Professionals interested in transitioning into the aviation sector.

Program Requirements

  • Participants come from different sectors and industries
  • Proficiency in spoken and written English is necessary

How You Benefit

Develop a comprehensive understanding of airline operations, airport management, aviation regulations, and global industry frameworks.
Apply aviation safety and security principles to enhance operational efficiency and regulatory compliance.
Analyze airline revenue management strategies, including pricing models, financial planning, and cost optimization techniques.
Evaluate airport and airline operational practices related to sustainability, human resources, crisis management, and service quality.
Strengthen strategic thinking, leadership, and decision-making skills required for supervisory and management roles in aviation.
Gain internationally recognized IATA certifications alongside an AUB certificate to enhance employability and professional credibility.
Build valuable industry connections through interaction with aviation professionals, instructors, and peers.
Position yourself for career growth within airlines, airports, aviation authorities, and related organizations.

High Diploma in Public Law

Chairman–Director General, Lebanese Civil Aviation Authority

Appointed on 17 July 2025 as Chairman–Director General of the Lebanese Civil Aviation Authority. Joined MEA in 1972 as a cadet pilot and served until 2016. Held multiple senior positions including Safety & Compliance Manager at the MEA Training Center. Contributed extensively to IATA, ICAO, and AACO committees on operations, safety, human factors, and IOSA oversight. Represented Lebanon on ICAO panels and served as an expert for ICAO conducting audits and delivering aviation security training.
