Program Description

The program provides essential knowledge and practical skills required to succeed in aviation management, safety, and operations, along with industry-recognized credentials. Integrated content in aviation operations, safety & security, airline and airport management through theory and real-world applications.

Combining IATA-certified training with AUB academic expertise, the program delivers a practice-oriented learning experience that prepares participants for supervisory and leadership roles while supporting sustainable growth of the regional aviation sector.

Learning Objectives & Outcomes

  • Develop understanding of safety, security, and operational frameworks ensuring global regulatory compliance.
  • Apply safety and security protocols to mitigate risks and enhance operational safety.
  • Analyze and implement airline revenue optimization strategies: cost control, financial management, pricing.
  • Evaluate airport and airline management including HR, sustainability, crisis management.
  • Utilize industry data and analytics for informed managerial decisions.
  • Navigate complexities of air transport industry and international aviation law.
  • Communicate effectively with stakeholders: airlines, airports, regulators.

Course Descriptions

AUB Aviation & Airport Management: Airline operations, airport planning, aviation law, HR, sustainability, crisis management.
IATA Aviation Security Awareness: Security regulations, industry threats, access control, response measures.
AUB Airline Revenue Optimization: Financial management, pricing strategies, cost control, revenue management techniques.
IATA Aviation Safety Fundamentals: Safety Management Systems (SMS), risk assessment, human factors, safety culture.
